Bridging the gap between you and financial institutions with trust and transparency.

Welcome to Mallu Finance, your trusted Kerala-based gateway to secure financial solutions. We understand that navigating the world of loans and banking can be overwhelming. Our mission is simple: to connect the people of Kerala with reputed, RBI-regulated banks and financial institutionsâ€”with 100% transparency and zero cost to you.

Who We Are

Mallu Finance is a dedicated financial service platform. It is important to note that we are not a bank or a lending institution. We do not provide loans directly, nor do we manage your financial applications. Instead, we act as a secure bridge, guiding you to established, legal platforms where your financial needs can be met safely.

Our "Customer-First" Promise

We operate on a foundation of integrity and Malayali hospitality. To ensure your peace of mind, we strictly follow these principles:

  • No Fees: We never seek money from our customers. Our services are completely free for you.

  • No Sensitive Data Collection: We do not ask for your OTPs, original documents, or private credentials.

  • No Data Storage: Your privacy is our priority. Mallu Finance does not save your PAN card details, contact numbers, or email IDs in our database.

  • Secure Redirection: Any data you enter is processed directly on the official, legal platforms regulated by the RBI and the respective banks.

  • Transparent Earnings: Our platform is sustained solely by commissions provided by the banks. We will never ask a customer for a service fee.

Why Choose Mallu Finance?

Based in Kerala, our sole intent is Customer Service. We bridge the gap between complex banking systems and your financial goals. By using our platform, you ensure that you are dealing only with legitimate, regulated entities, protecting you from unverified and predatory lending apps.
